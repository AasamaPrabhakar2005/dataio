Welcome!

Groups of 1-4 will work on projects relating to actionable insights, data visualization, and/or AI and predictive algorithms. Datasets will be provided by our sponsors. Judges will award prizes for best overall project, best visualization, and best insights as well as for each sponsored challenge. Mentors will be available to help throughout the day. This is a great event to get more data concentrated practice than at a case competition and to have something shorter than a hackathon, while still collaborating with peers, professors, and professionals.

Sponsors

Without our sponsors and supporters, Data I/O wouldn't be possible. By joining us, you'll be in touch with some of the best developers, designers, and builders in the state. Our team will ensure that you have an incredible experience.
